Laputa's Stew (from Castle in the Sky)

Description

We can't forget the delicious looking stew that Sheeta cooks up for her sailor crew in this Ghibli classic, Castle in the Sky. Sheeta makes a warm and hearty winter vegetable stew that combines earthy and sweet flavors - perfect for fueling an upcoming adventure to an unknown land! Even though she uses no meat, the vegetables that absorb the dashi stock give this dish a very savory flavor; however, feel free to add meat or any other vegetables that you like!

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need:

    For the broth:
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 3/4 cups dashi stock
    For the veggies:
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 potato
  • 1/2 onion
  • A handful of shiitake mushrooms
  • A few chopped spring onions for garnish
    For the flavorings:
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Mix your dashi broth and water. In a separate bowl, mix all the soup flavorings together. Set aside.
  2. Cut all your veggies (carrots, potataoes, onions) into bite sized pieces. Finely chop the scallions.
  3. Cut off the stems of the shiitake mushrooms and cut all the heads in half diagonally.
  4. Heat up a pot and add the olive oil. Then add the onions and stir for about 2 to 3 minutes until the edges become translucent.
  5. Add the carrots and potatoes. Continute to stir for about 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Add in the mushrooms then continue to stir for another 1 to 2 minutes.
  7. Add the broth into the pan. The broth level should come above the vegetables, if not simply add more water or dashi stock until it does.
  8. Bring the water to a boil. Leave it boiling for a minute or two before bringing it back to a simmer. Add in the flavoring after bringing it to a simmer.
  9. After about 20 minutes of simmering, you're delicious, adventure-fueling soup should be ready!
