Ponyo Ramen

Description

For us, ramen is a dish best served….. literally anytime, with anything. But, there's just something especially tantalizing about the ramen shown in Ponyo, with its thin slices of bright pink ham, tasty egg and scallions… we're drooling just thinking about it. Luckily, this Ponyo ramen is one super easy dish to recreate from home. Just follow the steps below!

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need:

  • 1 package Nissin Chicken Ramen (or your favorite soupy instant ramen)
  • 1.5 cups boiling water
  • 1 slice cooked ham (cut in half) use a thick slice if available
  • 1 hard boiled egg
  • 1 chopped green onion

Instructions

  1. Boil the water.
  2. Place the noodles and seasoning packets in a ramen bowl.
  3. Pour boiling water over the noodles and cover. Set and start your timer for three minutes.
  4. After the first minute, quickly uncover, shake the noodles to separate, then arrange the ham, egg, and green onion over the noodles. Cover again until the noodles cook to how you desire.
  5. Slurp up your noodles, but don't make a mistake like Ponyo and make sure to wait for them to cool down!
